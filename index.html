About me

Born in Bologna and based in Milan, I grew up with a passion for design applied to computer science. Hours and hours spent programming, drawing UIs, designing UX and studying the best communication strategies led me, together with my studies, to actively participate in the Milanese design panorama. My path began at the technical institute with a computer science major, where I learned all the notions of programming and networks and the Internet. I specialized in an engineering course that then led me to the Faculty of Design at the Politecnico di Milano, one of the world's excellences in Design, where I still study and specialize. In my free time, I like to follow F1, run and program, my great passion.
